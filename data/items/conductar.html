Installation comissioned for Moogfest 2014

An immersive agumented-reality experience, layered on top of the city of Asheville, North Carolina where the Moogfest festival takes place. The attendeed are invited to wander the city and conduct a generative audio-visual world through movement and their neurological response to the environment.
