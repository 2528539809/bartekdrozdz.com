Simple game + sound visialization concept.

Move you mouse sideways to move the ship. Avoid rocks, collect gems. Models are exported from Unity 3D. Custom shaders create the "old video game" effect.

Track: Atari Dream by Wrap Monkeys

Technology: WebGL

You need a WebGL enabled browser run this experiment. Chrome or Firefox are the best choice. You can also enable WebGL on Safari on desktop.
