A game with sound visualization.

This little demo is an unfinished game. To play, move your mouse sideways to steer the ship. Avoid rocks, collect gems. Models are exported from Unity 3D. Custom shaders applied on top create the "old video game" effect.

Track: Atari Dream by Wrap Monkeys

Technology: WebGL

You need a WebGL enabled browser run this experiment. Chrome or Firefox are the best choice. You can also enable WebGL on Safari on desktop.
