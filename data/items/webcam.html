Bad reception effect of a webcam feed.

Playing with some shaders to create a bad reception effect. Move your mouse around (or tap anywhere on a mobile device) to change the effect and its intensity. For video, it uses the camera feed provided by the WebRTC API.

Note: Be sure to click Allow on the toolbar above.

Technology: WebGL, WebRTC

You need a WebGL and WebRTC enabled browser run this experiment. Chrome on desktop and Android is currently the only browser to support those both APIs.
