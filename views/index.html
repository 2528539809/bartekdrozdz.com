I am an experienced Web Designer/Developer living in Los Angeles, California.

For the last 12 years, I've been working in the field of interactive development in both Europe and the US. I have colaborated with renowned interactive agencies such as F-i, B-Reel, North Kingdom and Tool of North America.

I create high quality websites, interactive experiences and games in technologies ranging from HTML/CSS to WebGL, Unity3D and even things like Raspberry PI.

In my free time, I like to experiment and learn everything from new technologies and APIs all the way to layout and responsive design.

The projects I contributed to have been widely recognized in the industry and received multiple awards, including:

  • 1 Emmy
  • 1 FWA PCA
  • 14 Cannes Lions
  • 3 FWA Site of the Month
  • 3 Awwwards Site of the Month
  • 16 FWA SOTD/Mobile

Occasionaly, I speak at conferences and other events as well as conduct workshops.
