About

I have 12 years of experience in coding and designing interactive content.

I worked in Europe and US for several renowned interactive agencies, including F-i and North Kingdom. Currently I work at Tool of North America in Santa Monica as Creative Technologist.

The projects I contributed to have been widely recognized in the industry and received multiple awards, including an Emmy, 14 Cannes Lions, an FWA People Choice Award, 3 FWA Sites of the Month and numerous others.

In my free time, I like play and experiment with web technology and everything around it. Occasionaly I speak at conferences and other events.
