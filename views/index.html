I'm a creative director working on interactive projects.

Photo by Robert Lindstrom

I run a design+development studio in Los Angeles, California. We bring to life digital creative ideas and turn them into interactive experiences, apps and games. We use technologies such as CSS/Javascript, WebGL, Unity3D, node.js (and beyond!)

For the last 12 years, I've been working in the field of interactive development in both Europe and the US. I have colaborated with renowned interactive agencies such as Psyop, Tool of North America, North Kingdom and B-Reel.

My projects have been widely recognized in the industry and received multiple awards, including:

  • 1 Emmy
  • 1 FWA PCA
  • 14 Cannes Lions
  • 3 FWA Site of the Month
  • 3 Awwwards Site of the Month
  • 23 FWA Site of the Day/Mobile
  • 10 Awwwards Site of the Day

In my free time, I like to experiment with interactive design & technology. My experiments have been featured on such websites as Chrome Experiments and Creative JS among others.

Occasionaly, I speak at conferences and conduct workshops on creative coding. I'm a member of the jury at the Awwwards.
